Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026

NASTY is a family board game app. It doesn't have accounts, ads, analytics, or trackers, and it doesn't sell or share your data with anyone — there's nothing to sell, because it barely collects anything.

What stays on your device: your saved game (so you can close the app and resume later), your game settings (sound, animation speed), and your personal leaderboard (games played and won) all live only in your device's local storage. None of it is sent anywhere or visible to us.

Online multiplayer: if you host or join an online game, the moves you make (which card you played, which tee you moved) and the name you type when joining are relayed through a small server the developer runs, only so that everyone at the (virtual) table sees the same board — the same way a dealer at a real table would. That's it: no profiles, no chat logs kept beyond the current game, no advertising use, nothing sold to third parties. Rooms and their history are automatically deleted after they've been idle for a while.

If you ever have a privacy question, email blake.pangman@gmail.com.
